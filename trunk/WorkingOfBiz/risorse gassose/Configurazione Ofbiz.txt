Per configurare Ofbiz e Mysql:
andare in Ofbiz/framework/entity/config ed editare "entityengine.xml" e inserire: (ricordarsi di modificare nome utente e password"
<datasource name="custommysql"
            helper-class="org.ofbiz.entity.datasource.GenericHelperDAO"
            field-type-name="mysql"
            check-on-start="true"
            add-missing-on-start="true"
            check-pks-on-start="false"
            use-foreign-keys="true"
            join-style="ansi-no-parenthesis"
            alias-view-columns="false"
            drop-fk-use-foreign-key-keyword="true"
            table-type="InnoDB"
            character-set="latin1"
            collate="latin1_general_cs">
        <read-data reader-name="seed"/>
        <read-data reader-name="seed-initial"/>
        <read-data reader-name="demo"/>
        <read-data reader-name="ext"/>
        <inline-jdbc
                jdbc-driver="com.mysql.jdbc.Driver"
                jdbc-uri="jdbc:mysql://localhost/ofbiz"
                jdbc-username="ofbizuser"
                jdbc-password="mypass"
                isolation-level="ReadCommitted"
                pool-minsize="2"
                pool-maxsize="250"/>
        <!-- <jndi-jdbc jndi-server-name="localjndi" jndi-name="java:/MySqlDataSource" isolation-level="Serializable"/> -->
    </datasource>
    <datasource name="custommysqlolap"
            helper-class="org.ofbiz.entity.datasource.GenericHelperDAO"
            field-type-name="mysql"
            check-on-start="true"
            add-missing-on-start="true"
            check-pks-on-start="false" 
            use-foreign-keys="true"            
            join-style="ansi-no-parenthesis"
            alias-view-columns="false"
            drop-fk-use-foreign-key-keyword="true"
            table-type="InnoDB"
            character-set="latin1" 
            collate="latin1_general_cs">
        <read-data reader-name="seed"/>
        <read-data reader-name="seed-initial"/>
        <read-data reader-name="demo"/>
        <read-data reader-name="ext"/>         
        <inline-jdbc                  
                jdbc-driver="com.mysql.jdbc.Driver"
                jdbc-uri="jdbc:mysql://localhost/ofbizolap?autoReconnect=true"
                jdbc-username="ofbizuser"
                jdbc-password="mypass"  
                isolation-level="ReadCommitted"
                pool-minsize="2"
                pool-maxsize="250"/>               
        <!-- <jndi-jdbc jndi-server-name="localjndi" jndi-name="java:/DerbyDataSource" isolation-level="ReadCommitted"/> -->
    </datasource>
dopo la seguente riga: "<datasource name="localmysql"
poi aggiungere:
<delegator name="default" entity-model-reader="main" entity-group-reader="main" entity-eca-reader="main" distributed-cache-clear-enabled="false">
    <group-map group-name="org.ofbiz" datasource-name="custommysql"/>
    <group-map group-name="org.ofbiz.olap" datasource-name="custommysqlolap"/>
</delegator>
al posto di quello di già c'è.
Inserire il driver connector mysql in "Ofbiz/framework/entity/lib/jdbc" e in Mysql creare da root lo schema "ofbiz" e lo schema "ofbizolap".
Poi eseguire ./ant rin-install nella cartella di Ofbiz per configurare il DB.

CREARE DB in OFbiz e Mysql per la nostra applicazione:
Ipotizziamo di avere un applicazione "Practice" sotto la cartella "hot-deploy". Bisogna creare una cartella "entitydef" in cui creare due modelli di entità che rispecchiano il DB che vogliamo creare: "entitymodel.xml" e "entitygroup.xml". Bisogna poi aggiungere le seguenti linee al file "ofbiz-component.xml" che si trova nella cartella dell'applicazione (prima della direttiva <webapp>):
<entity-resource type="model" reader-name="main" loader="main" location="entitydef/entitymodel.xml"/>
<entity-resource type="group" reader-name="main" loader="main" location="entitydef/entitygroup.xml"/>
riavviare il server di Ofbiz e controllare se nel file "console.log" che si trova in "hot-deploy/runtime" ci sono le seguenti linee:
5336  (main) [            UtilXml.java:242:DEBUG] XML Read 0.0070s: /home/sichen/eclipse/workspace/ofbiz/hot-deploy/practice/entitydef/entitymodel.xml
5693  (main) [            UtilXml.java:242:DEBUG] XML Read 0.0050s: /home/sichen/eclipse/workspace/ofbiz/hot-deploy/practice/entitydef/entitygroup.xml
